About Me

I am a Computer Science graduate student pursuing a double degree between Politecnico di Milano and the University of Illinois Chicago. My research interests lie at the intersection of computer vision, deep learning, and 3D scene understanding.

In Summer 2026 I will be joining the Princeton Vision & Learning Lab as a Visiting Research Collaborator under Prof. Jia Deng, working on robust 3D scene reconstruction from multi-view images. Incoming

Previously I worked as a Research Engineer at EssilorLuxottica Smart Eyewear Lab collaborating with Ray-Ban Meta on quantized model deployment for embedded smart glasses, and as a Machine Learning Engineer Intern at BeMyEye, building deep learning systems deployed at scale for the Schwarz Group (Europe's #1 retailer).

I am broadly interested in 3D computer vision, neural network efficiency, federated learning, and autonomous driving perception. Feel free to reach out!
